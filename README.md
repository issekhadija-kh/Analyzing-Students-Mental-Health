# Analyzing-Students-Mental-Health

This project analyzes the relationship between the length of stay and mental health diagnostic scores among international students. The goal is to understand how the duration of time spent aboard may influence average scores across three different mental health assessments.

## Objective
Explore and analyze student data to determine how the length of stay impacts the average mental health diagnostic scores of international students.

## Expected Output
A summary table with 9 rows and 5 columns, filtered to international students only, sorted by length of stay in descending order and lastly rounding all average values to two decimal places.

## Columns:
• `'stay'`: length of stay(years).
• `'count_int'`: Number of international students for that length of stay.
• `'average_phq'`: Average PHQ-9 score, rounded to 2 decimal places.
• `'average_scs'`: Average SCS score, rounded to 2 decimal palces.
• `'average_as'`: Average ASISS score, rounded to 2 decimal places.
